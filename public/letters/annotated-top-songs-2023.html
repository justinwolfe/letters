  1. "bo$$ chick" by saweetie (preview)

this is one of those songs where there's one specific little part that just hits so hard for me, which is when she says, the instruments all dropped out, "what's that in my cup that's that potion" and then the kick hits

pure joyful energy. cannot count the number of times my brain has earwormed "jesus CHRIST / i'm bout to sin again". the song is so short and action-packed that i feel physically compelled to start it again after it ends.
