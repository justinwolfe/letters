i'm thankful that on our lunch break yesterday, i reserved some of miso's food and took her to the field by the school so we could practice her following along on her leash, rather than doing what she often does, which is at a certain point deciding that she will not be moved and standing still for a long time until you pick her up and reposition her elsewhere. i'm thankful to know rome wasn't built in a day and i'm thankful that when we were there the sun was out.
