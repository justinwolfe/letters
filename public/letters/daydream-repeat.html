at lunch discussion of the group's favorite musicals (top three: chicago, the phantom of the opera, wicked) to which my answer was of course "none" but also there was a hilarious moment where deborah was sure that we had watched sweeney todd, a movie i have definitely not seen, together, and i figured out eventually that it was just that we had just watched that episode of the office where andy is in sweeney todd so many times (for transparency, i will note that recently i was wrong and deborah was correct about us having watched the movie burning and about last night's episode of true detective being the finale, which i didn't think it was supposed to be but it was (and it was truly truly ridiculous the spooky version of the song "twist and shout" and the part where they quote the line "time is a flat circle" are the shark hitting the moon from jumping so fucking hard)).

(office reference: the episode where michael falls into an indoor koi pond)
