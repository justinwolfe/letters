i'm thankful that rather than continue to look at an annoying error message or go to the trouble of actually moving files and managing my icloud storage, i paid $.99/mo to up the storage to 50 gigs from 5 gigs so that i don't have to deal with this problem. i'm thankful for any problem i can resolve by spending $.99/mo.
