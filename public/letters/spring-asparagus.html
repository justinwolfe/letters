spring asparagus

April 25, 2024

things i've been watching: the zone of interest (now streaming on max if you missed it, haunting and i appreciate jonathan glazer as a director who is still actually trying to do some shit formally, even if the movie felt like more of a tone piece), fallout (superb no notes and (unfortunately) brought into further relief for me how truly terrible the netflix adaptation of the three body problem really was; i regret trying to hype you up for it). the jerrod carmichael reality show (i wish people were talking about this show more, every new episode effortlessly doing hard stuff that nobody else on tv would even think to try), couples therapy (we've watched all the seasons now and are bereft with the loss of it), bands on the run (classic vh1 reality show dvd rips uploaded to youtube by some minor saint)

things i've been reading: china mieville's book about the communist manifesto (great, much more readable than his book about the russian revolution, which first thrilled and then bored me in its granularity), the waste land: a biography of a poem (lots of funny/interesting trivia (eliot was such a freak! also anti-semitic!) but i wanted more of a sustained close reading of the poem to go with it)

things i've been listening to: "good luck, babe!" by chappell roan (i did not like "casual" (which deborah loved) and liked "pink pony club" at first but ultimately found it annoying but i really really love this song, which i described to a friend who's in the know as Tennis-pilled), "get it right" by bnny (why yes i do love to gaze at my shoes), ""wanna be" by glorilla with megan thee stallion (always here for new good feeling myself-core, also particularly love "white boy wasted / channing tatum" line in the chorus), "hold me up (thank you)" by khruangbin (it sounds like all their other songs that i like but honestly they can just keep doing that same thing forever and it's fine because they do it so good), "una vida pasada" by camilo and carin leon (came across it during a weekly new music friday trawl and it's my new "bubalu", an aural encoding of the vibes i'm trying to live as spring blooms)

things i've been eating and drinking: honey mango, grillo's pickle flavor kettle chips, tequila cocktails deborah has been making with fresh citrus juice, a reese's snack cake left in the fridge too long and consumed greedily in a moment of weakness upon discovery, jeni's sweet cream biscuits and peach jam ice cream (hadn't had it for a while and confirmed still a top 2 ice cream for me), smashburgers with sweet potato "fries" and a brussels sprout and apple salad (shave some brussels sprouts, dice some green apple, slice a little onion, mix and then dress with some improvised combination of vinegar, honey, mustard, salt, pepper, and spices)(i was describing the recipe to deborah so she could execute it and using "some" to describe the proportions of things and she said that if i had a cookbook every recipe would just be "some this" and "some that"), sundubu in a stone pot (the stone pot is soooo worth it also frozen mixed seafood dumped in), garlic stem kimchi, spring asparagus
