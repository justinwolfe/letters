one pot mushroom rice, adapted from cook with dana: https://cookwithdana.com/one-pot-mushroom-rice/#recipe makes 2 servings

  1. soak 1 cup rice in 2 cups water, in the rice cooker pot
  2. meanwhile, slice:
    • 1 giant portobello mushroom (quartered, then sliced) (i would have used shiitake but i couldn’t find any at the store)
  3. mince:
    • 1 tbs ginger (grated frozen, didn’t mince)
    • 3 garlic cloves
    • 1 shallot
  4. wash rice until it rinses clean (about 4-6 times). drain all water
  5. add to the rice:
    • 3/4 cup water
    • 2 1/2 tbs soy sauce (i will add 1/2 tbs fish sauce to this next time, the recipe calls for a dark soy sauce too but i couldn’t find it)
    • 1 tbs oyster sauce
    • 1 tsp sugar
    • 1 tbs olive oil
  6. cook in rice cooker (white rice setting)! should take around 45 min
  7. slice 1 green onion
  8. air fry 2 chicken thighs (400ºF for 12 min, salt & pep, flip, season again and cook for 8 minutes)
  9. shred chicken
  10. divide rice into two bowls, add chicken, add green onions

cucumber salad, adapted from a tiktok recipe i found when looking for din tai fung cucumber salad

  1. mix:
    • 2 tbs soy sauce
    • 1 tbs rice vinegar
    • 1 tbs honey
    • 1 tbs gochugaru
    • 1 tbs sesame seeds
    • 1 tsp sesame oil
    • 2 green onions, sliced
    • 1 clove garlic, minced
  2. slice 1 cucumber and combine with the sauce (i usually slice the skin off, halve the cucumber length-wise, slice into half-moons) (usually u use persian cucumbers but i just used a regular one)
